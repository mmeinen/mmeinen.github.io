Inferno Meadow

Inferno Meadow

An in-browser turn based strategy game. Drawing on games like Advanced Wars and Fire Emblem for inspiriation this game relies on critical analysis to conquer the enemy. Play in either a hotseat style with the enemy next to you or play solo against the fearsome AI! Good luck!

Narwhal Bomber

Narwhal Bomber

A Windows 8 App chain reaction game. Set off an explosion and watch it propegate through all the narwhals! Each level gets progressively harder, there more narwhals the more you need to detonate. Collect all the horns!

Punch Force

Punch Force

The silliest of phone apps there ever was. Exclusive to Windows Phone, hold on to your phone and punch! It will then tell you how hard you punched. See which of your friends can punch the hardest!
